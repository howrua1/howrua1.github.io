Pain Points of Installing Addons on Kodi 17

Are you having difficulty installing addons on Kodi 17 or facing errors while adding them? Do you find the process of installation too complicated and time-consuming? Do not worry; we have you covered. In this post, we will simplify the process and make it easy and accessible for all users.

How to Install Addons on Kodi 17?

The installation of addons on Kodi 17 is a straightforward process. Follow the instructions given below:

  1. Open the Kodi 17 application on your device.
  2. Click on the “Add-ons” option available on the left-hand menu.
  3. Select the “Download” icon, and a prompt window will appear.
  4. Select “Install from repository” and choose the add-ons and repository you want to install from the list provided.
  5. Select the addon and click on “Install” to download it.

That’s it! After installation, you can open the add-on from “My Add-ons” and stream your favorite movies and shows instantly.

Summary of How to Install Addons on Kodi 17

In summary, the process of installing addons on Kodi 17 is straightforward. You need to go to the “Add-ons” section, select the repository and addon you like, and click on the “Install” button. Finally, open the addon from “My Add-ons” and enjoy your streaming experience.

How to Install Third-Party Addons on Kodi 17?

If you want to install third-party addons on Kodi 17, follow the instructions given below:

1. Open Kodi 17 application.

2. Click on “Settings” icon or gear-shaped icon on top-left corner.

3. Select “System Settings” > “Add-ons” > “Unknown sources”.

4. A prompt window alerting you about the risks of enabling unknown sources will appear.

5. Select “Yes” and confirm your choice.

6. Go to the “Add-ons” section and select “Install from zip file”.

7. Browse and select the addon ZIP file you want to install.

8. Wait for the installation to complete and select the addon from “My Add-ons” to use it.

That’s it! Now you can install any third-party addon on Kodi 17 using the above steps.

How to Update Addons on Kodi 17?

To update your installed addons on Kodi 17, follow the instructions given below:

1. Open your Kodi 17 application.

2. Click on the “Add-ons” section and select “My Add-ons”.

3. Go to the addon that you want to update.

4. Click on the “Update” button.

5. Wait for the update to install, and the new version of the addon will replace the old one.

That’s it! Keep your addons up to date and enjoy your streaming experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Why can’t I install addons from the official Kodi repository?

A1. The official Kodi repository does not have all the addons available on the internet. You may need to install third-party addons to access more content.

Q2. Why do I get errors while installing addons on Kodi 17?

A2. Sometimes, Kodi 17 may not support certain addons. In such a scenario, you may need to update Kodi 17 to its latest version or reinstall a fresh copy of Kodi 17.

Q3. Can I install addons on Kodi 17 from a popular repository?

A3. Yes, popular repositories like Kodil, SuperRepo, Colossus, and Noobs and Nerds have a wide range of addons to choose from.

Q4. Can I install addons on Kodi 17 using a ZIP file?

A4. Yes, you can install addons on Kodi 17 using a ZIP file by selecting “Install from zip file” in the addons section.
