Installing Sims 4 Mods

If you want to enhance your gaming experience, you can install Sims 4 mods. These mods are user-created modifications and add-ons that change the gameplay. To install mods, you need to download and unzip the respective mod files and then copy them to the Mods folder located in “Electronic Arts>Sims 4>Mods”. Ensure you enable the Mod option in the game, and the mods will appear in the game.

How to Install Sims 4 House and Lots

If you want to install Sims 4 House and Lots, you need to download them from third-party websites such as The Sims Resource and Mod The Sims.
