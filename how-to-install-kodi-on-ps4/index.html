Answering the Target: How to Install Kodi on PS4

Before downloading Kodi on your PS4, you should have a PlayStation Network account. After that, you need to follow these simple steps:

  1. Open PS4 and click on the PlayStation Store.
  2. Search for “Kodi” in the search bar.
  3. Select the Kodi app located in the search results.
  4. Click on the “Download” button, and the installation of Kodi on PS4 will begin.

After the installation is complete, you can launch Kodi by going to the “TV and Video” section of your home screen.

How to Update Kodi on PS4

Updating Kodi on your PS4 is a straightforward process. Once you have installed Kodi, you can update it to the latest version by following these steps:

  1. Open the Kodi app on your PS4.
  2. On the home screen, select the gear icon.
  3. Select “system” from the menu on the left-hand side.
  4. Click on “System Update” and let it update to the latest version.

It’s essential to keep your Kodi updated as it ensures a better streaming experience and keeps the app bug-free.
