How to Install Kodi on Amazon Fire TV Stick

Before we start, make sure you have a stable internet connection. Here are the steps to install Kodi on Amazon Fire TV Stick:

  1. Go to “Settings” on your Fire TV Stick.
  2. Select “Device” and then “Developer Options”.
  3. Turn on “Apps from Unknown Sources” and “ADB Debugging”.
  4. Click on “Home” on your Fire TV Stick remote.
  5. Click on the search icon and search for “Downloader”.
  6. Click on the “Downloader” app and install it.
  7. Open “Downloader” and type in the URL: http://www.kodi.tv/download.
  8. Download the Android version of Kodi and wait for it to install.
  9. Click on “Open” to launch Kodi.
