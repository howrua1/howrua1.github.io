Installation of Kodi on Apple TV 3rd Generation

The process of installing Kodi on your Apple TV 3rd generation is not as complicated as it may seem. Follow these simple steps:

  1. First, you must convert your Apple TV into a jailbroken device. This is the only way to install Kodi on Apple TV.
  2. Download the latest version of Kodi for Apple TV through Cydia Impactor or Nito Installer.
  3. Connect your Apple TV and computer on the same Wi-Fi network.
  4. Open the Cydia Impactor or Nito Installer tool that you downloaded earlier and select the Apple TV.
  5. Drag and drop the Kodi deb file that you downloaded onto the Cydia Impactor or Nito Installer tool.
  6. Enter your Apple ID and password when prompted.
  7. Wait for the installation process to complete on your Apple TV.
  8. Once the installation is complete, you can access Kodi from your Apple TV’s home screen!

It’s that easy! Now you can enjoy endless hours of entertainment on your Apple TV 3rd generation.
