Answering the Target of How to Install Kodi on Firestick with Voice Remote

To install Kodi on Firestick with voice remote, you need to follow these simple steps:

  1. Go to the settings option on your Firestick
  2. Click on My Fire TV and proceed to Developer Options
  3. Turn on Apps from Unknown Sources and ADB Debugging
  4. Return to the home screen and search for the Downloader app in the search bar
  5. Download and install the Downloader app
  6. Open the Downloader app and input the Kodi download URL; https://kodi.tv/download
  7. Download the latest version of Kodi that is compatible with FireStick
  8. Click on Install and wait for the installation process to complete
  9. Click on Open and launch the Kodi app on your FireStick.

With these simple steps, you can easily install Kodi on Firestick with voice remote.
