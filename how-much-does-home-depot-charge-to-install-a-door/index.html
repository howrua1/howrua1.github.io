The Pain Points of Door Replacement

Door replacement is not rocket science, but it’s not always as straightforward as it seems. You need to choose the right type of door that fits your home’s style and ensure it’s installed correctly. Without proper installation, the door cannot perform its safety and security functions, and you may end up spending more money to fix the problem. Moreover, removing an old door by yourself can be challenging, time-consuming, and dangerous if not done correctly.

How Much Does Home Depot Charge to Install a Door?

The average cost of installing a door at Home Depot ranges between $60 and $600, depending on several factors such as the type and size of the door, labor, and materials. Most customers spend around $220 to install a standard door. At Home Depot, door installation costs include the cost of removal and disposal of pre-existing doors (if applicable), professional installation, any required carpentry or modifications, and all associated materials and labor.

Summary of Key Points

So, in summary, Home Depot charges around $60 to $600 to install a door, including the cost of door removal, professional installation, any necessary modifications, and materials and labor. Door replacement can be a daunting task, but Home Depot saves you the hassle and gives you peace of mind by providing quality service.

Expert Tips

If you want to replace a door, consider choosing a door that’s weather-resistant, insulated, energy-efficient, and durable to add more value to your home. Measure the door frame before purchasing and have someone assist you with installing the new door. If you’re opting for a more complex door installation, like a storm door or double security doors, call Home Depot to speak with an expert for an appropriate quote that’s tailored to your specific needs.

The Cost of Additional Features

Home Depot charges an additional fee to install features such as locks, handles, and deadbolts. The cost of installation for knobs and locks starts at $30 per piece. For more complex locks, like smart locks or keypad locks, installation costs can range between $100 to $200. For this reason, it’s essential to know precisely what you need before asking for a quote.

Budgeting for Your Door Installation

Door installation is a worthwhile investment, and it’s essential to budget for the project. FAQs About Home Depot Door Installation

1. Does Home Depot install all types of doors?

Yes, Home Depot installs all types of doors, including interior, exterior, storm, and patio doors.

2. Does Home Depot charge to measure doors?

Home Depot offers free door measuring as part of their installation services.

3. Does the cost of door installation include painting?

No, the cost of door installation does not include painting. Home Depot offers painting services at an extra cost.

4. How long does it take for Home Depot to install a door?

The installation time varies depending on the type of door, home structure, and modifications required. On average, a standard door installation takes about 4-6 hours.
