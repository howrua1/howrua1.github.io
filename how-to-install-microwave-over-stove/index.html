How to install a microwave over stove

The first step in installing a microwave over your stove is to gather all the necessary tools. This includes a drill, measuring tape, screwdrivers, and the mounting brackets that came with your microwave. Before beginning the installation process, it’s important to carefully read the manufacturer’s instructions and follow them closely. This will ensure that your microwave is installed correctly and safely.

Next, you’ll need to determine the exact location where you want to install your microwave. Measure the distance between the stove and the bottom of your cabinets to ensure that there is enough room for the microwave to fit. Once you’ve determined the location, mark it with a pencil so that you can easily identify it during the installation process.

It’s important to note that this is not a one-person job. Enlist the help of a friend or family member to assist with holding the microwave in place while you drill and secure the mounting brackets.

Securing the mounting bracket
